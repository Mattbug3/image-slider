Dune
Adapted from the classic sci-fi novel, it follows the young Paul Atreides as he navigates political intrigue and battles for control of the desert planet Arrakis, home to the coveted spice melange.
How to Train Your Dragon
Follows the adventures of a young Viking named Hiccup who befriends a dragon and works to bridge the gap between humans and dragons in his village.
Batman
Depicts the vigilante Batman's crusade against crime in Gotham City, balancing his dual identity as billionaire Bruce Wayne.
Avatar
Transports viewers to the lush alien world of Pandora, where a paraplegic ex-marine becomes involved in a conflict between the indigenous Na'vi people and human colonists seeking to exploit the planet's resources.
Harry Potter
Follows the journey of a young wizard named Harry Potter as he attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and battles against the dark wizard Voldemort.
Joker
Offers a chilling portrayal of the iconic Batman villain, delving into the psyche of Arthur Fleck as he transforms into the infamous Joker amidst Gotham City's societal decay.
Call Me by Your Name
Chronicles the summer romance between Elio Perlman and Oliver, an older graduate student, set against the backdrop of the Italian countryside in the 1980s.
